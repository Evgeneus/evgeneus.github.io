Hello! I work as a post-doctoral researcher at Multimedia&Human Understanding Group, Italy. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Trento, where I worked on finite pool classification in identifying items that pass a set of filters via crowdsourcing, hybrid crowd-machine learning, and active learning algorithms.

During my work, I use a variety of machine learning approaches from TF-IDF to Word Embeddings and Transformers, from Logistic Regression to Deep Neural Networks and Graph Networks. For obtaining high-quality training data from possibly noisy sources, I employ state-of-the-art crowdsourcing and data fusion algorithms.
