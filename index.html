Hello! I work as a post-doctoral researcher at Multimedia&Human Understanding Group, Italy. My current research interests span over Graph Neural Networks for Computer Vision, Domain Adaptation, and Self-supervised Learning, in particular, when little or no supervision is available. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Trento in 2020, where I worked on finite pool classification in identifying documents that pass a set of filters via crowdsourcing, hybrid crowd-machine learning, and active learning algorithms.

During my work, I use a variety of machine learning approaches from TF-IDF to Transformers, from Logistic Regression to Deep Neural Networks (CNN, RNN) and Graph Neural Networks. For obtaining high-quality training data from (possibly) noisy sources, I employ state-of-the-art crowdsourcing and data fusion algorithms.
